Planning Tips

  • Bundle classifications: If multiple components share an HS8 code, request an advance binding ruling to lock in the declared rate for the full shipment.
  • Balance cost vs. speed: Air freight offers rapid replenishment but spikes landed cost; rail can be a middle-ground for EU-bound orders with moderate urgency.
